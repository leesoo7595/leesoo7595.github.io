### 백준 2588번


```python
first = input()
second = input()

temp = []

for i in range(0, len(second)):
    temp.append(int(second[i]) * int(first))

temp.reverse()
temp.append(int(first)*int(second))

print('\n'.join([str(x) for x in temp]))

```
